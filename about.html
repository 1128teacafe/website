Business Goal

The 1128 Tea and Cafe aspires to excellence, character, and quality in everything we do.

Product - Providing everyone with the best quality and most interesting
varieties of coffees, teas, and accompaniments is our main goal.

Service - It is our intention to treat our customers with respect as our
guests; we are committed to providing prompt, efficient, friendly, and
relaxed service to all our customers.

Atmosphere - Our coffee house will be maintained in a clean, attractive
environment at all times and will provide a pleasant, welcoming atmosphere.

Work Environment - 1128 Tea and Cafe is committed to providing a great working
environment for its employees. Creating a positive and respectful work environment
as well as empowering our employees to be themselves and achieve their
full potential are our primary goals.

Community - We are committed to advancing the spirit and well-being of our
neighborhood and the community as a whole.

Products

The unique milk tea and coffee house environment offered
by 1128 Tea and Cafe makes it unlike any other in Daet.
The cafe is more than just a place to pick up your daily
cup of coffee. It's a place to sit down and enjoy a coffee
made to your specifications. We have a place set aside for
you to sit if you need to work. With our professionally
trained baristas, we provide a variety of delicious drinks
made from authentic ingredients at an affordable price.
From traditional coffee to our own house-made specialty
drinks, we have it all. The ingredients used in our products
are all made in-house with all-natural ingredients, ensuring
that they are of the highest quality in terms of taste and health.
It will be a pleasure to serve you at 1128 Tea and Cafe!

Partners

We like to say that we are not in the tea & cafe business
serving people, but in the people business serving tea &
coffee. Our employees – who we call partners – are at the
heart of the 1128 experience. We are committed to making
our partners proud and investing in their health, well-being
and success and to creating a culture of belonging where
everyone is welcome.
